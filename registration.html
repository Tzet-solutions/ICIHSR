Registration

At least one unique author of each accepted paper must register on or before 15th January 2024 for the papers to be included for publication. The participating delegates should submit the registration form, copyright form, scanned copy of payment receipt and the camera-ready paper by email to icihsr@kmeacollege.ac.in on or before 31st January 2024.

Registration Fee

  • PG/UG Students - 3000
  • Research Scholar/ Academicians - 4500
  • Industry Delegates - 3500
  • Participants - 1000
  • Foreign PG/UG Students- 100 USD
  • Foreign Research Scholar/ Academicians - 200 USD
